{{ 'Causes You Can Help' | translate }}

  • Red Cross - Ayeyarwady Delta

    "The Red Cross is mapping the Ayeyarwady Delta area in Myanmar as part of a multi-year mapping and data readiness activity to better understand where critical infrastructure and roads are to inform decision making during potential disasters. As recently as 2008 a cyclone killed at least 77,000 people with over 55,900 missing, and left about 2.5 million homeless."

    Matt Gibb, Red Cross

    Find Ayeyarwady Delta Projects

  • Tanzania Development Trust

    "Tanzania has a high incidence of extreme poverty, early marriage and Gender Based Violence. NGOs on the ground need better road and residential area data to facilitate their outreach work. Community mappers from a HOT microgrant, together with YouthMappers from the Institute of Rural Development Planning will map employment opportunities as part of a project to reduce youth vulnerability starting on 26th August. This base map will assist with this field mapping."

    Janet Chapman, Tanzania Development Trust

    Find Tanzania Development Trust Projects

  • Ongoing Disaster Mapping

    "With our disaster mapping program, what we are trying to provide is a common operating picture for the first responders, aid organizations, governments and the affected people to make informed decisions. When all parties are working with the same information, the better situated the community is for quick and effective recovery."

    Russell Deffner, Humanitarian OpenStreetmap Team
